The Pillars of Emotion are physical pillars that store memories of all souls in the omniverse. These pillars exist inside the domain of Homestead at its center. The Pillars exist in a circle above the calm sea that reflects the lights of beautiful and bright stars above. Only 8 pillars exist. The pillars that exist are the following with their color.

- “Nada”  Hope (White)
- “Trist” Sadness (Blue)
- “Srecan” Happiness (Green)
- “Strah”  Fear (Black)
- “Tazione” Might (Yellow)
- “Szal” Madness (Red)
- “Ponos”  Pride (Orange)
- “Kauns”  Regret (Purple)
