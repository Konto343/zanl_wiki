# The 5 Elders
*Created: (~February ?, 2023)*

The Elders are the oldest things to ever exist. Elders control all of existence behind and in the megaverse. They can control existence without existing themselves. No soul. No form. Elders will only do something if it threatens their “existence” or the megaverse. They sit around the megaverse in a group of 5.
