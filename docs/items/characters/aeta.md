A being who represents a pure soul and is wise to conflict. Wants peace. Brother of Necrodon. 

Aeta is an entity created by the Figure. Aeta along with his brother were made to balance out the opposing forces of the omniverse. They save and destroy those around them. Aeta is an entity who is white in their true form. Aeta has a crown which is composed of “shards”. This crown is completely white. Aeta shines and glows in the color white. Entity discovered humans after birth and wanted to save them. And not just humans, everything in existence. He savored existence and the hopes of other people. So he takes the jobs of canceling out all bad forces in the omniverse.

He created a cat named Enra. Enra is a cat that scouts all the evils in the cosmos looking for dangers. She currently resides with Bex, thinking he’d be a good hope for saving the omniverse one day. She only likes the food he gives her.

Aeta can appear in his true form or as a human in disguise. He made the form so he could talk to humans in a friendly manner. Aeta has powers equal to Necrodon, but his power exceeds everything in existence. His power allows himself to alter reality, fiction, concepts, space, and time. But he can achieve more beyond that. As for weaknesses, he is defined with none. He is a power that can't be rivaled but the likes of anyone. That is except for his creator. Aeta’s goals are to take down the largest of evil forces in the omniverse and restore peace. He wishes to eradicate all those who oppose the good and innocent.

## Story:
Who am I? Who are you? Why do I exist? What is my purpose?

I lay in this space amongst the stars. Are these stars my creator? But these pillars surround me. Each one of them glows brightly with a color of sensation. Each color represents a feeling of one’s past. As I place my hand on these pillars, I find out these feelings are memories of my past. Happy, sad, and angry are some to name. But these memories don’t feel like mine. I don't remember them either. I wish to understand them and learn from them. But for now shall I answer one question at a time. 

I reside on top of the sea, but there happens to be an invisible floor that prevents me from interacting with the water. I look up to see the shimmering stars. All so beautiful. But it's not enough to conclude where I am. I see a white door afar. I approach it to stare at its white handle. But how come I know these things even though I just woke up? I knew nothing, but I know what “stars”, “sea”, and “doors” are. 

I conclude I have the ability to discover the identity of existent matter at sight. I look at the floor to see my reflection. It’s me.** **What am I?** **I appear to be a figure of sorts. A human. A human who is lost? A human from what? How was I born here? This goes nowhere where I question myself. I appear white. As white as those stars above. As bright as the door I see across. I don’t like such glowing appearance. So i’ll make myself more to my desire. So little I know to change my look. Should I learn, I look into these pillars. 
I found out what is called “life”. This life exists on a giant spherical shape of mass called a “planet”. These masses are bound together by an odd force called “gravity”. Such a planet out of all million in existence is called “earth” where this life takes place. This planet had been born through the extreme compression of positive and negative spaces. With positive overruling negative, such a huge finite space was created. This overruling shouldn’t be possible, as the equal amount of positive and negative matter would’ve collapsed into nothingness. But such an event had happened out of something else’s influence. That something being out even my own knowledge. To what I had been saying earlier. 

This life on this earth blooms out after millions of years. The planet had once a rock, which had compressed, which quickly heated. So it cooled after millions of years to give birth to “water”. This water filled much of the planet’s deep shape. The bright star that allows this earth to move is called, “sun”. The sun is a bright star that gives off a warm color. This color carries the heat inside light. This water and the sun give creation of tiny figures called “organisms”. These organisms are bound together to create larger masses. Some grow differently and in many different ways. The earth grows with the earth and gives birth to life. Such life consists primarily of “forests”, “trees” “birds”, “dogs”, and “humans”. The old dominant life was called “dinosaurs”. These creatures roamed the earth and moved across the earth and its waters. Thus, until a huge mass of “rock” came to collide with the earth. This made it glow brightly and left the planet in “flames”. The ground was in ruin with the dinosaurs all “dead’. After millions of years, once again give birth to a smaller species now called “humans”. These humans are interesting figures as they grow. They conflicted with “wars”, and cherished each other with “love”. This balance of war and love grew into “evolution”. This sparks the progressive climax of this species. Today the earth sits peacefully with no such important events happening.

Only slow and diverse changes to the “structure” that this species has set up. But where I stand, I understand how “humans” works. To change my current situation, humans have many different approaches to appearances. They also have many different “ideas” and “dreams” appearances. So I'll follow their ways to match my own desire to appear. A piece of fabric that clothes the back of its user is called a “cape”. Such an item appeals to me. A dark craft of “wool” and “leather” is known of two items. Human’s call them “pants” and “shirts”. A dark combination of clothes shall mimic the dark space that surrounds the stars. I will put a craft for the two bottom ends of my figure, I believe humans call them “shoes”. Now the “skin” of my figure shall be created. Humans vary between white and black. I’ll have mine be a peach of sorts. Close to the color of the stars. Two types of humans exist, female and male. I’ll decide to have none of the “reproductive organs” they called. But my appearance shall be that of a man. This is because there are more male than females on that planet. A cape looks to cover only the back, so I'll get its alternative. The one that covers the back and “head” of my figure. They call it a “cloak”. Now for the top. I have kind of a swift black “hair” on the top of my figure. I’d like to do something to cover this hair. Humans have created from their environments a “hat”.

This had as many other pieces of self can come in many different ways and “styles”. A kind of hat I'd want is a type that has space and height. So a “top hat” would satisfy. A subtle mark would indicate my ownership. I’ll place it on the bottom crease from the top. Now something to protect myself against the “evils” of this world. A long stick of hard materials would bear grip to my “hands”. A staff would be my desire. The surface is without shape or detail. Shall I engrave it with a pattern of life? A pattern that should hold similar to that of the pillars. A dark metal shall be its material. It reflects the light of those bright stars.

A companion is those called “animals” or “pets”. I would pick the one opposite of my “person” type and those called “dogs”. A cat. A female cat that reflects all water and starlights. Shall you accompany either me or those in need of help. Protect those in name with all the strength of the stars. In case of worry shall I explore and wait for a human of worth and hope.

A name. A name is what humans give to physical matter to give importance to it. Thus, shall I name myself, Aeta. This shall mean “All. Empathy. Trust. Abjure.” to mean that all I trust and empathize for should hear me abjure. I care for all that is kind and hopeful. And for my companion, shall I name you “Enra”. Lastly, my staff. I’ll give the name “Sunignol”. For the meantime I shall reside in a sleeping state, waiting for the time to give hope.
