A red haired woman who had a strong hatred against her father and his terrible ruling. Now serves as the leader of a huge army in charge for control peace.

Her full name is Kendra Rae Baymad. She rules and commands over an entire army called the “Genesis”, an army feared among the cosmos.

## Story:
Long ago on a distant planet named Dama, an old kingdom ruled over half the world. Having the strongest warriors on the planet, a kingdom stood to command over of the nations. Global conflicts were solved at the hands of this kingdom. It had name known or feared by the people, Gohel. Gohel had controlled peace and challenged those who dare hate their ways. After winning a major war between an equally large nation, The king and queen bear a child. A child whose hair appeared orange at birth. A female who was eventually name Kendra. She grew up under the care of her parents and lived a life of luxury and war. A rebellion form in the world to turn over Gohel due of their mistreatment of smaller nations. The invaded and enslaved people weaker than them and covered it up so no one discovered their secrets. With every battle won, Kendra discovered little more of the truth. 

At the age of 13, Kendra discovered and pieced all the crimes and evil her father did to the people. She soon hated her father with extreme hatred. Her mother was the only one she felt comfortable around. Kendra spread the truth to the people and her mother. The queen came to disagree with the king’s actions, but the king had only exiled his child and wife to the Rebellion, seeking both to die alone. They wondered for miles along the snowy terrain until a group of people. They approach the child and queen with their guns pointed at them. The men recognize the people as members of Gohel, and curse them for doing those wretched things to them. Kendra’s mother tries to convince them that they never those thing were happening. The leader of the group points his gun at her head, but Kendra steps from behind her mother to tell the men something. They are convinced after she tells her own story and the hard truth. Feeling bad, the men take the women and child. They treat both of them with food and medicine. Kendra and her mother are introduced to the Rebellion and are told of the truth. They grow sad for the two and what others have been through. So with their guidance, the Rebellion grew in size and became stronger. 

At 21 years old, she had taken a role as commander in the Rebellion. The group took forth from their grounds to Gohel. Marching with all their might assisting the lower states. The states became part of the Rebellion and expanded their forces, Gohel tried to sent their own power to stop the growing Rebellion, but they were quickly out strategized. Kendra used her gathered expertise to command the men into the right position at the right times. They grew fighting Gohel with their might and power. Her father grew in worry that they would take them, so he decided to send a large group of soldier to ambush a Rebellion camp. Smokes run afar in the sky show that they are wake. The surround the area and scout for any guards. They find nothing and they wait. The commander gives his order and the base is ambushed all of is raided. They looked for 30 minutes to find nothing left behind. It was decoy to put them off. 

Meanwhile near the Gohel kingdom, where multiple groups of Rebellion soldier lie outside the walls of the kingdom. They climb the outer walls and take any guard while not alerting anyone. Infantry look around for entrance points while the Rebellion quietly moves around. They all gather around the castles walls in a calm matter. While they are preparing to invade the castle, a guard notices them moving around and rings alarm. Kendra has no choice to fight the castle’s men. They swiftly take on the soldiers taking little loses as much as possible. Taking down the side doors of the castle, they flood in coming in crossfire within the massive palace. Grenades and bullets flew around. The castle starts to crumble under the fighting. Men take over floor one of the place and head up to King’s room. Guards are placed everywhere and not moving from their spots. Five men head forward, not thinking about what would happen. Their heads are quickly chopped off from the large guards. This was problem they couldn’t solve. The bottom, top, and side of the king’s room were covered in guards. Its was impossible to enter. The amount of people they currently was triple that of the Gohels, but their skill levels are completely different. Since their was no way get to the king’s room, the only choice Kendra had was to wait. To take over the surrounding area was going to benefit them in short term. They keep the first floor of the castle in check, while maintaining the area around them. 

They rested for 2 days, gather soldiers from other nations. On the day of the evasion, they gather around every staircase to the upper floor. Kendra gives the signal and all hell breaks loose. Weapon fire releases across the place and swords clash. The had lost 26% percent of their men in the first 8 minutes. The guards all defeated and they reach the King’s room. Kendra enters the room alone. She sees her father siting in chair look out a window. The appears to old in age and skinny. His white hair stretches down past his shoulders. “Daughter, I’ve missed you.” he says. Kendra with disappointment, “You’ve missed me? How could leave us to sit in cold to suffer! You wanted us to die!” The old man turns to look at her, “I know I done many wrong things, yet I can’t make up for all of them.” Kendra angrily replies, “Make up? You’ve continued to attack us even during all of this years! You haven’t changed even slightly from when you exiled us!” The old attempts to get up, “Even this old man wouldn’t be able to kill his own daughter. I’ve taken over, leaving me to rot here. One of my generals had replaced me and I’ve been here for over 8 years now. What I did to you and your mother is indeed incorrect. After I had exiled you, I was in despair, wanting you to come back. I went with a party to look for you, but you made it too far. I searched for as long we revisit the cold weather. I had let my emotions get to me, and I failed to correct what I did.” Kendra slaps the man, “Its only excuses! Even you tried to save us, you gave up! That shows how much failure you are.” They sit in silence looking away from each other. Someone knocks on the door, “Come in”, Kendra says in frustration. Two men walk in, “We are here to report that the entire kingdom has been secured.” one man says. “No other forces have been seen so far. The remaining forces from the castle have been capture.” the other man says. “Thank you, you can leave to your stations.” Kendra says. She stares at her father sitting in his chair afar and leaves the room.

The sun arises. 11 years have passed. With a discovery of a powerful material that conducted a strong amount of energy, the worlds goes through many technological advancements. After 2 years when Gohel was taken down, a stone called “Jectium” was found in a deep mine. This stone was tested on and proved to be great power generator. It could also store and freely emit energy. After some heavy experimenting and designing. It was the flow energy could be controlled and emit in a certain direction. A prototype miniature fusion reactor was made to power a floating device. It would use energy from the reactor to emit energy downwards, creating lift. Not even the engineers can still explain how it works. And with that, flying cars made. Advanced weaponry to protect against any threat are made. The world population spikes with better and healthier environment. Organizations are made and peace treaties are formed across the globe. Everyone lived good lives with more many jobs be given to people. The larges cities with their tall skyscrapers brim with light as flying vehicles fly by. A tower that stands the tallest can be seen. At the very top of this top tower is the General. A general who commanded entire fleets and armies at the wave of her hands. Protecting the galaxy and cosmos from any dangers to the people. She looks up at the sky and its stars, “Mother, I hope you are proud. I do all this for you.” she thinks. 2 Months after capturing Gohel, her father had passed. She was is still unsure how truthful he was to her. “Forgiving my father for doing the things he did isn’t easy.” she thought. Her mother had fallen old of age and was dying. So at her passing, Kendra didn’t feel sad but rather determined. So with her leadership, she guided her armies across my battles and won all of them. No one shall ever forget the name of those protected the world, “Genesis”.

## Origin: 
Comes a Star Glitcher game on Roblox. Star Glitchers were scripts or code in Roblox that a person could “exploit” into the game to create destruction. The injected a piece of code into the game client and allowed the user to run code onto the server and the client. Basically hacking the game. I found Star glitchers cool because of the music player and their appearance. Their were “mode” you could switch between in the star glitcher that had different attacks and music. Each track of music went along with how the user would appear. For example if a song with violence or strong beats player, the stronger and harsh looking the user appeared. The user’s wings and appearance would match a color that music’s genre would represent. Red being hardcore or “death” sounding. Blue being quick and energetic. Purple representing corruption and torment. Rainbow for a relaxing or an 8-bit video game feeling. Black would be death and destruction. White was equality and justice.

To the point, Baymad’s name comes after a star glitcher’s mode name, called “Bayham”. I replaced the “ham” part with “mad” and made “Baymad”.
