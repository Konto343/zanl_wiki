A smart man who is able to handle physical and mental fights with ease. Is strong and hates his father due his obsession for his army.

Calyale Zango is the oldest brother of the Zango family. At a young age, Calyale was known for starting drama and many fights. He was a smart man, so he had won both physical and mental fights. 

## Story:
People who dared argue with him, would’ve been laid down with many psychological tricks. With the Onzal army at its peak, Calyale’s father had offered a position in the army and wanted him to fight. “Fight with us for the best in the world! C'mon Calyale. This is a once in a lifetime chance.” the father had said. Calyale had grown tired of his own father. This is because he found his father to only care about war and battles. About how if he won them, it would change the world. His father dreamt of a perfect cosmos where all would fall to his terror and bow their heads. He would “rebuild the universe for the better”. Calyale turned down his father and he wanted to live a peaceful life with no distributions. He wanted power as well, to control those who opposed him. The power of his own to fulfill his desires. To live without limits and bothersome people. 

Amongst the world of demons and devils, Calyale was born, being hated for the trouble he caused. But as he grew, he understood what it took to harness power. So he fought hard and studied for nights. His intellect and strength finally satisfied him on a beautiful morning. He woke to look back on what he had done and felt happy. He reached his peak, and he was going to its fullest. Calyale traveled to the Shadow Realm. The Shadow Realm was just the back face of the planet they lived on. That side never got light, since the front side of the planet always pointed to their local sun. Most powerful and insane demons laid underground in the realm. 

Calyale sought a strong challenge to keep up with his progression. He went to layer zero, where the strongest demon laid, Kezel. Kezel is the most fearsome and strongest demon amongst all. At the doors that held this monster, many skulls were scattered everywhere, posing as a warning to trespassers. Calyale remained unfazed and approached the huge gates and knocked them back with a flick of his finger. Red eyes glow bright in the dark to greet Calyale. “It’s been a while since a person has been down here.” a deep voice echoes across the room. “But sadly, you’ll just be like the rest. Now you will die.” the voice says as a giant hand comes from the shadows to grab Calyale and throw him to the wall in a quick motion. He didn’t expect him to be this powerful. But this only acted as some finally good entertainment. He leaps off the wall and lands on the ground. The creature reveals itself to Calyale in the light. Calyale remains not harmed. There’s not even a scratch on his clothes. “Oh? You are not like the rest, are you? Well then. Time for me to put in some effort for once.” the gigantic demon says to pull out his dark blade.  They clash and claw for hours until Calyale gets the upper hand and slices Kezel’s head clean off. 

He leaves the room and throws Kezel’s head on the surface of the Shadow Realm. Demon stood in fear, but Calyale left too quickly for anyone to know who really killed Kezel. But that wasn't it. Calyale on the next day had killed all the monsters of layer one all the way to layer 8. The only things that knew of his terror were the ones that got killed. But their death prevents them from speaking of the truth.
