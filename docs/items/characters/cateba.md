A succubus-like woman who is able use her own body to murder men up to no good. Willing to help murder people who oppose her father's deeds and her own. Very manipulative and convincing.

Cateba Zango is the oldest sister of the Zango family. 

She has a loving appearance but don’t let deceive you. She always carries a coffin so when you die, your body will be ready to be stored.
