# Despair
*Created: I Forgot*

Despair is all evil discarded by Eternity, placed into the omniverse, acting as counter balance to Eternity's creationfullness.

This being grew its hunger outside its range of normal matter and focused on life forms and distant planets. Each week it grow more and more powerful, eventually able to sense an otherworldly presence. Moving at faster than the speed light, it arrived at a white heaven. However, it strange ringing echoed across the space, and white sun emitted behind the figure.

It was already to late, [he](/docs/items/characters/aeta) was there to dissolve it. Thus, two soon celestal siblings were born
